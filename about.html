In May, a group of young professionals gathered for a Bourbon Tasting Kick-Off event, and Louisville Mashed was born.

Join a group of young professionals eager to develop a deeper appreciation of bourbon, network with fellow bourbon-ites, and raise awareness for an important cause.

Organize a group of friends (teams of up to 5 people can compete, along with your favorite b ottle of bourbon, and enter it into the blind tasting competition. Guests will have the opportunity to taste and rate each bourbon at the event. At the end of the event, scores will be added up adn a winner will be chosen. The top 3 winning teams will take home great prizes!
