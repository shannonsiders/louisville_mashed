What is Louisville Mashed?

In early 2015, a group of young Louisvillians asked themselves a simple question.

"How can we get more young people interested in the spirit we all love: bourbon?"

From this humble query, Louisville Mashed was born.

Our vision for the event is to create a premier bourbon tasting experience with three goals:

  1. Help young professionals develop a deeper appreciation and understanding of Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey;
  2. Network with up-and-coming leaders in Louisville;
  3. Give back to our community.

This annual event will unite each of the goals into one fun night.

Cheers!

- The Louisville Mashed Committee
