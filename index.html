Bourbon

Compete in a bourbon tasting and drink delicious cocktails. Learn about bourbon from some of the best distillers Kentucky has to offer. Not a fan of bourbon? That's okay. We'll have beer and wine, too. Or you can just come for the company.

Community

Help support Family & Children's Place. Give children in the Louisville area a brighter future, free from abuse. Last year, nearly 3,000 families benefited from professional counseling, support and treatment at Family & Children's Place. All proceeds from Louisville Mashed will benefit Family & Children's Place.

Networking

Join young professionals from the Louisville area for a night of bourbon, community and networking. Are you a not-so-young professional? No worries, we dont discriminate, as long as you're ready for an evening of fun!
