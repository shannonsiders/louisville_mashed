Family & Children's Place has supported Louisville-area families for more than 130 years. Over the years, the organization has evolved to respond to children and families in crisis.

Family & Children's Place gives families in our region fast and easy access to comprehensive, seamless support as they face serious challenges. The organization strives to protect and heal children and families.
